Our School

Our Story

Norwegian International School has over 30 years’ experience in providing top quality international education in Port-Harcourt. Along with academic rigour, strong tradition and innovation, we equip our pupils with the ability to be well-balanced citizens and leaders of the world.

NIS has a rigorous academic standards which ensure that students are well prepared all–round the school year. At every stage, a pupil’s education is carefully planned. Pupils are active participants in the learning process and their achievement is monitored to ensure success and understanding. This results in consistent excellent Cambridge IGCSE and Checkpoint results.
